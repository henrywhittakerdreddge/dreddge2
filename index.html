Dreddge sources and develops class actions against misuse of personal data.

At minimum, half of our net returns will be donated to an independent, not-for-profit entity dedicated to research and direct action on personal data and privacy.

Working with a range of litigation funders, we are developing cases in six jurisdictions in Europe and North America. Each case is designed to maximise recovery for claimants. We also develop strategy and bespoke technology to gather statistically robust evidence of data misuse.

Based in London, our small team uniquely combines experience in litigation, litigation funding, data privacy law, and internet technology. We also draw on a global network of experts in these areas. Please get in touch if you’d like to join or learn more about us.
